About Swipe

Swipe is a public-facing nutrition and wellness utility for students, campus visitors, and the general public at the University of Connecticut. It provides real-time dining hall menus, AI-powered health scores, allergen warnings, and favorites tracking — all in one fast, easy-to-use mobile app.

  • Real-time menus from all UConn dining halls and retail locations
  • AI health scores (1–100) based on nutritional content
  • Allergen filtering, dietary preference settings, and favorites
  • No university login or affiliation required — open to everyone

Open to Everyone

Swipe is a publicly accessible application. No UConn NetID, university account, or institutional affiliation is required to download or use the app. Anyone — students, families, campus visitors, or members of the public — can access dining hall menus and nutrition information freely.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

1. Information We Collect

Swipe collects only the minimum information needed to provide a personalized experience. We do not collect your name, email, or any account credentials.

  • Dietary Preferences & Restrictions — Allergen flags, dietary tags (vegan, gluten-free, etc.), and food favorites you configure in the app. Stored entirely on your device.
  • Usage Analytics & Session Replays — Anonymous data about feature usage, app performance, and session replays (visual recordings of how users navigate the app) to help us improve the experience. Session replays do not capture text input or personal data. We use Sentry for crash reporting and Vexo for analytics and session replays.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Personalized Alerts — Your dietary preferences are used locally on your device to surface allergen warnings, filter menus, and notify you when a favorite item is available.
  • App Performance & Improvement — Anonymous usage data helps us identify crashes, optimize load times, and prioritize new features.
  • No Marketing or Tracking — We do not use your data for advertising, behavioral profiling, or any purpose outside of operating the app.

3. Data Storage & Sharing

  • Local Device Storage — All personal preferences, favorites, and cached menu data are stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage. This data never leaves your device.
  • Server-Side Cache — Dining hall menus are fetched and temporarily cached on our servers (Supabase) to ensure fast load times. No personal data is stored server-side.
  • No Third-Party Selling — We do not sell, rent, trade, or otherwise transfer any user data to third parties under any circumstances.
  • Deleting Your Data — Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data from your device. To request server-side data deletion, contact me at seanhoward02@icloud.com.

4. Children's Privacy

Swipe does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app is intended for use by university students, campus visitors, and adults.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
